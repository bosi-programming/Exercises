SELF-CHECKOUT

Create a simple self-checkout system. Ask for the price and quantity of three
items. Calculate the subtotal, the tax value using a tax rate of 5.5% and the
total.

Example:

Enter price of item 1: 25
Enter the quantity of item 1: 2
Enter price of item 2: 10
Enter the quantity of item 2: 1
Enter price of item 3: 4
Enter the quantity of item 3: 1
Subtotal: $64.00
Tax: $3.52
Total: $67.52
